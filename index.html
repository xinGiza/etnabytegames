Titles from the Studio

SILVARN

Jacob arrives at his family's home after they plead for rescue from unexplained deaths. He discovers the house empty — scattered belongings, signs of struggle. A psychologically unstable man convinced residents are cursed has taken them. Jacob soon discovers a mysterious elevator that connects random locations. He must navigate through this strange mechanism while being relentlessly pursued by the killer Silvarn through dark corridors and uncertain escape routes.

A relentless killer forces you to keep moving as you search for answers. Collect clues, solve disturbing puzzles, and uncover the terrifying truth hiding behind the fear.

The Deathmind

"You are not lost in a village. You are lost in your own mind."

Trapped in a forgotten village, you search for a missing wife — only to uncover echoes of a life that may never have existed. Unravel memories, decipher cryptic notes, and navigate a deteriorating psyche across three atmospheric chapters. Puzzles like Upside Down and Death Cycle challenge perception itself as the line between reality and madness dissolves.

A psychological horror game where environmental puzzles and fractured memory collide to reveal an unsettling truth.

About

Etnabyte Games

Etnabyte Games is an independent game studio founded in 2025 in Antalya, Türkiye. The studio develops all of its games independently, focusing on atmospheric and psychological experiences. It has released two titles so far: SILVARN and The Deathmind. The company aims to expand into future online and multiplayer projects while continuing its independent development approach.
